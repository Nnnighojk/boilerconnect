About {{ SETTINGS.PROJECT_NAME }}

Purpose:

This portal serves to facilitate the development of sustainable partnerships in service-learning between faculty, staff, students, and community partners. The portal does three things:

  • Allows community partners to share meaningful projects that will contribute to social and systemic change in the community.

  • Connects community partners to faculty, staff, and students through service-learning courses.
    •
  • Provides faculty and staff with an opportunity to develop curricula that will match the need of community partners.
    •


    Features:

    As a Community partners, you can go onto our website and submit a project or service request that directly addresses a need for your organization. As a Faculty Member, you can see these requests, and connect your students to this Community partners through a service-learning course partnership. Additionally, the site administrator will monitor and facilitate these partnerships.


    Creators:

    This website was created by Purdue students in the Information Management Systems (IMS) sub-team of Engineering Projects in Community Service (EPICS) in partnership with the Office of Engagement and the United Way. For questions regarding the portal, please contact: Lindsey Payne, paynel@purdue.edu. For more information on EPICS and IMS, please visit:
